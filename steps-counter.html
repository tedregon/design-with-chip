Track your daily steps and stay motivated to reach your fitness goals. Steps Counter helps you monitor your activity levels throughout the day with an intuitive interface and helpful reminders to keep you moving.

Why I'm building this app?

Health and fitness tracking has become increasingly important in our daily lives. I wanted to create a simple, focused app that helps people stay active without the complexity of larger fitness platforms.

Steps Counter is designed to be your daily companion for staying active, with clean design and essential features that make tracking your steps effortless and motivating.
