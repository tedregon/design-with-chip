Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

No Data Collection

The app does not collect any information from users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal information (such as name, email address, or contact details)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Crash reports
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

All functionality operates entirely on the user's device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services, software development kits (SDKs), analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies that could collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. As no data is collected at all, there is no differentiation based on age.

International Use

This app is made available globally. Because no personal data is collected, stored, or processed, no personal information is transferred across borders.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect those changes. Continued use of the app after an update indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
