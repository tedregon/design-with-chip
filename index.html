Why you're not getting calls

After 12+ years in product design — across startups and billion-pound businesses — I see the same 3 issues repeatedly:

No visible impact

You describe what you did.
But not why it mattered.

No measurable outcomes.
No business reasoning.
No clear ownership.

Design roles require business impact clarity.

Confusing storytelling

Your case studies read like documentation or marketing brochures.

Too much detail in the wrong places.
Not enough clarity around the problem, constraints, trade-offs, and decisions.

Hiring managers scan quickly. If the signal isn't sharp, they move on.

Lack of intention

Designers don't just execute.

They:

  • Frame problems
  • Identify trade-offs
  • Drive decisions
  • Align stakeholders

If your portfolio doesn't show intentional product thinking, you won't be invited to interviews.
