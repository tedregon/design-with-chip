Why you're not getting calls

After 12+ years in product design — across startups and billion-dollar businesses — I see the same 3 issues repeatedly:

No visible impact

You describe tasks instead of outcomes. Without business reasoning or clear ownership, you fail to demonstrate the value and results that senior design roles demand.

Confusing storytelling

Your case studies read like documentation, not strategy. By burying the problem and trade-offs in irrelevant detail, you lose hiring managers who need a sharp, clear signal.

Lack of intention

Designers must do more than execute. If your portfolio doesn’t show you framing problems, driving decisions, and aligning stakeholders, you won’t be invited to interview.
