What I offer

I provide personalized coaching sessions focused on helping product designers improve their chances of getting hired. Together, we'll work on:

  • Portfolio review and optimization: Get feedback on your case studies, presentation, and storytelling to make your work shine.
  • Interview preparation: Practice design challenges, portfolio presentations, and behavioral interviews with targeted feedback.
  • Career strategy: Develop a clear plan for your job search, identify the right opportunities, and position yourself effectively.
  • Resume and application materials: Polish your resume, cover letters, and LinkedIn profile to make a strong first impression.
  • Design skills development: Identify gaps in your skillset and create a roadmap for growth that aligns with your career goals.

Who it's for

This coaching is designed for product designers at any stage of their career who want to improve their hiring prospects. Whether you're:

  • Breaking into product design from another field
  • Looking to move to a better role or company
  • Struggling to get interviews or offers
  • Wanting to level up your presentation and communication skills
  • Seeking guidance on navigating the job market

How it works

We'll start with a conversation about your goals, current situation, and what's holding you back. From there, I'll create a customized coaching plan that addresses your specific needs.

Sessions can cover portfolio reviews, mock interviews, resume feedback, career strategy discussions, or focused skill development—whatever will have the biggest impact on your job search.
