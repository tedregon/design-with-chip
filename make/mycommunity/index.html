Feel less alone by turning intention into action. Group the people who matter and get a weekly nudge to reconnect — one person from each circle. Meet in person, hop on a video or phone call, or send a thoughtful text, then log it in seconds. Connecting with people on a regular basis, will help you develop a sense of belonging, and bring back that sense of community many of us have lost, ultimately helping you feel less lonely.

Why I'm building this app?

After spending over 8 years away from family and friends, I felt like I lost a part of me. I made new connections, but I did not know what it took to make those connections matter. The pandemic made me feel even more isolated.

After researching loneliness, how people connect, and what it takes to build a sense of belonging, I made a system for myself that helps me connect with friends and family. The system worked for me so well, that now I feel present and intentional with my community. I wanted to share this strategy with the rest of the world, to help as many people as I can to feel less lonely.
